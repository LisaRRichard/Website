Minerals

Vancouver Island has a diverse landscape and geological . There are many treasures to find if you only look! Mid Island is plentiful in the islands stone Dallasite. In Victoria and area this has all been picked over and collected unless you're lucky. On beaches with sandstone cliffs you can find fossilized sea creatures and shells. Rivers often have concretions that may have awesome fossils of crabs, ammonites or other ancient creatures. There is plenty of fantastic finds on the beaches such as beautiful Jaspers, agates and flowerstones. Happy Hunting! Click my button to see samples of finds!
